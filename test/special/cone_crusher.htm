    A:Hydraulic cone crusher can crush medium hard materials, such as river stone, granite, basalt, limestone, quartz, diabase, iron ore, gold ore, copper ore, etc.

  • How much is hydraulic cone crusher?

    A:When cone crusher works, you should feed materials whose input sizes aren’t more than 85 percent of feed port’s dimension, or it will make the capacity reduced and parts damaged.
