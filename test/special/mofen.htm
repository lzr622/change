Feldspar

Feldspar is one of the most common minerals in the crust, which accounts for 60 percent of the earth's crust. Feldspar is main component of igneous rock, and containing potassium or sodium can be used in ceramics, glass and enamel industry.

Barite

Barite can be used as white pigment, and it is also applied in chemical, paper making and textile industry. When producing glass, barite can brighten glass as fluxing agent. Besides, the most important function of barite is applied in drilling industry as weighting agent and refining barium.
