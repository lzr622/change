With advantages of simple structure and easy operation, Ball Mill is tradition grinding equipment popular in the worldwide.

But many industries have put forward to higher requirements on capacity and output size along with the rapid development of industry and improvement of process.

Therefore, Liming Heavy Industry has launched MTW European Trapezium Mill,the ideal substitute product of Ball Mill, according to rich experience and advanced concept.

The capacity can reach 50t/h, which satisfies environmental protection requirements very well.

There is grinding roll linkage supercharging technology. The efficiencyis improved and capacity is increased by20%.

Bevel gear overall drive makes transmission ratio accurate and gearing chain reduced.The energy loss is reduced and efficiency is improved.

The surface can change the blade. The capacity and utilization ratio is improved at the same time rate of wear is reduced.

Air inlet volute without resistance and same resistance arrangement scheme makes pecuniary caused by system energy consumption reduced.

Novel internal oil lubrication system can lubricate bearing easily without oil pump or lubrication station.

Original curved duct can reduce airflow resistance and improve fan efficiency. It is difficult to block materials.

Dust emission: this machine is equipped with dust wiper. The dust removal efficiency can reach 99.9% and there is not almost dust in the workshop.

Running volume: new longitudinal pendulum structure makes grinding roller doesn’t contact with grinding ring directly. The noise is lower 25-30 decibels than ball mill’s.

MTW European Trapezium Mill

Type: Mineral Ore Powder Making

Input Size: 33-40 mm

With vertical structure, MTW European Trapezium Mil possesses small covered area and complete system. It consists of main mill, fan, classifier, dust collector, cyclone collector, pipe device and electric machinery. Auxiliary equipment consists of jaw crusher, bucket elevator, electromagnetic vibration feeder, hopper and power distribution cabinet.

MTW European Trapezium Mil is a closed circulation system running under the negative pressure. This machine is made of quality raw materials, and it is advanced grinding mill with advantages of durability, reliability and high degree of automation.
