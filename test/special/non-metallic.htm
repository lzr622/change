Non-metallic minerals can be used in metallurgy, construction, mining machinery and chemical industry by deep processing or fine machining. There are many kinds of non-metallic minerals including calcite, calcium carbonate, limestone, barite, gypsum, bentonite and so on, and they are processed into fine powder or ultra-fine powder by crushing and milling to make industrial products.

Adopting advanced technologies in Europe and combining innovative ideas, Liming Heavy Industry produces grinding plant professionally. And the equipment applying to non-metallic minerals has won many plaudits from global customers.
