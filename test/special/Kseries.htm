Working process

Based on two stage process and three stage process of stationary crushing line, mobile crushing plant can be combined in this way, too. Materials are sent to mobile jaw crusher for primary crushing, and belt conveyor sends them to secondary mobile crusher. Materials are crushed by cone crusher and then are sent to vibrating screen by belt conveyor. Materials satisfied requirements will be sent to the third mobile crusher for shaping, and unqualified materials won’t be sent to cone crusher for reprocessing until they meet the demand.
