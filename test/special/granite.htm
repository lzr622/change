700tph Granite Processing Line

is one of the large scale stone crushing projects. There are jaw crusher, hydraulic cone crusher and sand making machine as main machines, vibrating feeder, vibrating screen as auxiliary equipment. This line processes granite mainly.

Working Process

Dumper sends granite below 1020mm to vibrating feeder which will send these materials to jaw crusher immediately. Jaw crusher doesn’t crush granite primarily until the size ranges from 150mm to 300mm, and then vibrating feeder sends these materials to hydraulic cone crusher for medium crushing. Vibrating screen will screen granite over 40mm to go back to cone crusher for reprocessing, and materials below 40mm can go to sand making machine for shaping. After shaping and screening, belt conveyor will send materials to finished product area.
