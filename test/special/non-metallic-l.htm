Non-metallic mineral is important raw materials relating to metal ores, which include limestone, gypsum, granite, calcite, basalt, barite, feldspar, kaolin, bentonite and etc. materials. Non-metallic mineral is rich in variety, and it has a wide application used in construction, metallurgy, ceramics, glass, chemical industry, paper-making, rubber and machinery. With the modern industry develops rapidly, the types of non-metallic mineral will increase continually.

  • Limestone

    Limestone

    limestone can be used to produce glass, and hydrated lime can be used as coating materials and adhesive.

  • Granite

    Granite

    granite can be used in advanced building decoration project, hall ground and outdoor sculpture.

  • Calcite

    Calcite

    calcite can be used as rholite, and it can be also used to produce cement, lime, plastic, paper, toothpaste or food filler.

  • Barite

    Barite

    barite can be used in chemical industry, paper-making, textile filler, and it can be also used as fluxing agent in glass production.

  • Gypsum

    Gypsum

    gypsum can be used as cement retarder, building product, medical food additives, paper filler and paint filler.

  • Basalt

    Basalt

    basalt is good raw material of cast stone, and it can be also used as lubricant in cast steel production.

Processing method

01

Crushing process

in general, the process pulverizing materials into granules whose sizes exceed 1-5mm called crushing. Impact force is applied to the material to reach the state of fragmentation. There is two-stage crushing and three-stage crushing process. Jaw crusher crushes materials primarily, and impact crusher or cone crusher crushes materials secondarily, then sand making machine will shape materials, finally final products are got.

02

Grinding process

According to materials’ characters and specific requirements, some materials should be ground to suitable fineness. Depending on input size and output size, you can choose Raymond mill, high pressure suspension roller mill, vertical mill, super pressure trapezium mill, etc. machines.
