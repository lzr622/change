VSI5X Sand Making Machine integrates three crushing modes together. Materials moving at high speed will be crushed voluntarily because of friction,so not only can this machine crush stones, but also it will shape materials to some extent.

Central feeding:

this method helps materials get highest kinetic energy.

Crushing between materials:

the shape is good but power content is a little much.

Waterfall feeding:

the capacity can be improved when the power isn’t changed.

Crushing between materials and liners:

high efficiency, low costs.
